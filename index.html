Design

Our goal with this language was to provide a more conveniente way of writing regular expressions. With this language, you will get a more programmatically looking language with a simple yet powerfull syntax, increasing readability and mantainability of your future regular expressions!

Drop in replacement

REgify is not built to replace the entire Regular expression library for Python, just the anicent syntax for writing the regular expressions
