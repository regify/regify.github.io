Design

Our goal with this language was to provide a more convenient way of writing regular expressions. With REgify you will get a more programmatically looking language with a simple, yet powerfull syntax, which were developed with readability, usability and mantainability in mind!

Drop in replacement

REgify is not built to replace the entire regular expression library for Python, just the anicent syntax for writing them!
